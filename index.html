Hello World v.0.0.1

May 28th 2016

Personal things

Over the past few days I keep thinking what makes me what I am today — a lazy one liner. Having realized that I started everything early, I was blinded with the facade that I got a heads up. I became the cocky rabbit who got beaten by the turtle. Took me a while, but I’m done racing.

This time I will not run.

I’ll write.
